DÖMD 2013

DÖMD is a three days event with music, parties and one of northern Europe's largest amateur dart competitions with 128 competing teams which I arranged together with 12 other students. I was part of a three person graphics team and responsible for the marketing and graphics of the event. That involved keeping in touch with sponsors, creating prints, designing the website and mobile app as well as delivering within tight deadlines. The printings included advertising posters, a logotype, flights for darts, a folder about the event and stickers for beer bottles.
